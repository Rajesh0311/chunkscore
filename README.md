## What is ChunkScore?

ChunkScore audits the quality of your document chunks before they hit your vector database. Bad chunks = bad retrieval = hallucinating LLMs. ChunkScore catches the problem at the source.

## Why it matters

- 80% of RAG failures trace back to poor chunking strategy
- Embeddings are only as good as the chunks they represent
- Free chunking libraries (LangChain, Chonkie, LlamaIndex) chunk your documents — ChunkScore tells you if those chunks are any good

## Features

- Chunk quality scoring
- Chunking strategy comparison
- Import pre-chunked JSON arrays for quality audit
- Token estimation
- Export to Pinecone, Weaviate, Chroma, JSON, CSV
- API-first with full documentation
- Webhook support
